WHAT WE DO

Performing Arts Council (PAC) is a subcommittee under College Student Council (CSC) in Tembusu College. It aims to:

  • unify performing arts Interests Groups (IG) towards common goals;
  • provide aid to performing arts IGs in getting funds from internal and external sources;
  • cultivate performing arts culture in Tembusu College;
  • encourage group interaction and provide a forum for discussion within the student performing arts community;
  • provide technical support for all performing arts IGs and individuals





POSITIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

PRESIDENT

  • Organise and preside over all regular meetings
  • Represent the voices of PAC
  • Oversee the running of the council and the Abbey

VICE-PRESIDENT

  • Assist the President in daily running of the council and the Abbey
  • Gather and organize venues and performance preferences (Abbey bookings and equipment requests)
  • Respond to venue or scheduling conflicts or requests

SECRETARY

  • Oversee the organization and distribution of meeting agenda and document meeting minutes
  • Draft proposal letters to relevant administrators
  • Keep records of past events and achievements of all performing arts IGs
  • Help performing arts IGs to formalize their funding request by providing their past records as the supporting document

OPERATION DIRECTOR

  • Propose and help execute new projects (workshop, performance, seminar, etc.)

TECHNICAL DIRECTOR

  • Form and lead a technical team consists of 2-3 members to provide technical support for performing arts events within Tembusu College
  • Oversee the regular maintenance of the Abbey and its equipments

PUBLIC RELATIONS DIRECTOR

  • Update the website and the social network pages
  • Gather or consolidate information to promote the council, the performing arts IGs and performing arts events in Tembusu College

INNOVATION DIRECTOR

  • Maintain the proper running of the Abbey Booking System, Equipment Database and Online Record Logging System
  • Explore new opportunities in collaborating technologies and arts to serve the performing arts community in Tembusu College better
